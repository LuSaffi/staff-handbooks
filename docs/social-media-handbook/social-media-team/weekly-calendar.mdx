# Weekly Calendar

Based on the weekly schedule prepared by the **Event Team**, we prepare a weekly calendar for our social media platforms.

- The calendar template can be found in each week's folder and in the template folder:

```
\Eden Social\Week XX - XX-XX Month 2024 template
```

- The calendar must be prepared in **PNG** and **JPG** format by **Monday**.

:::note
Ensure the calendar is visually appealing and accurately reflects the upcoming events and posts.
:::
